Tàmbalee jàng Angale ak mbindu Wolof

A - E - I - O
Léral waxiinu araf yi: (ey) - (ii) - (ay) - (ow)
Misal 1: Apple (ni ñuy waxee: epël) -> Firi: Pomme (Garabu prut bu siiw bi)
Misal 2: Egg (ni ñuy waxee: eg) -> Firi: Nen
Misal 3: Ice (ni ñuy waxee: ays) -> Firi: Galaas (Ndox mu taye te am garaf)
Misal 4: Open (ni ñuy waxee: owpën) -> Firi: Ujji walla Ubbi
U - Y
Léral waxiinu araf yi: (yuu) - (way)
Misal 1: University (ni ñuy waxee: yuunivërsiti) -> Firi: Daara jolliti (Daara ju mag ju kawe ji)
Misal 2: Yellow (ni ñuy waxee: yelow) -> Firi: Kalar bu mboq
