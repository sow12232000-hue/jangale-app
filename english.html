Lëson 2: Sartu Boole Araf yi (Phonics)

Booleem araf yii buy jaxasoo di am bénn waxiin wu bees ngir gaaw a mën a lire :

1. Araf yi jaxasoo di am bénn kàddu (Digraphs)

Araf gi CH -> day nekk (c) ci Wolof: Chair (ni ñuy liree: cèer) -> Firi: Sées
Araf gi SH -> day nekk (sh / c bu rëy): Shop (ni ñuy liree: cop) -> Firi: Boutig
Araf gi CK -> day nekk (k) rekk: Duck (ni ñuy liree: dëkk) -> Firi: Kanaar

2. Voyelles yi booleexu di guddal waxiin bi

Voyelle gi EE -> day nekk (ii) bu tàllal: Tree (ni ñuy liree: trii) -> Firi: Garab
Voyelle gi OO -> day nekk (u) dëgër: Moon (ni ñuy liree: muun) -> Firi: Weer bi
Voyelle gi EA -> day nekk (ii) tamit: Meat (ni ñuy liree: miit) -> Firi: Yàpp
Lëson 3: Ay Misal yu baat yu guddu

Baat yii dañu guddu lool, waaye bu ñu sàmmé sart yi dañuy yomb a boole :

Beautiful (ni ñuy liree: biyuutiful) -> Firi: Rafet lool
Understand (ni ñuy liree: andërstand) -> Firi: Nand / Dégg / Xam
Important (ni ñuy liree: impòrtant) -> Firi: Lu am solo
