Lëson 2: Fraas yu am solo ngir Làkk (Everyday Phrases)

Tàmmal baat yii ngir mën a tàmbalee nuyoo ak jote ci àngale bës bu nekk :

1. Nuyoo ak Xame (Greetings)

How are you?
Nanga def?)
I am fine, thank you.
Maa ngi fi rekk, jërëjëf.
What is your name?
Naka la la tudd? (Naka nga tudd?)

2. Waxiin wu Rafet (Polite Expressions)

Nice to meet you.
Beg naa ci xam la.
See you later!
Ba beneen yoon! (Ba ci kanam!)
